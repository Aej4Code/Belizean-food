Land of the Free By Caribbean Sea

Belize is the forgotten Caribbean county rich in Mayan, Black, Garifuna, Indo-Asian and Mestizo cultures.


Belize just recently celebrated its 36th Birth of independence from the British on September 21st This country is composed of six districts: Corozal, Orange Walk, Belize, Cayo, Stan Creek and Toledo.
Being the only English speaking country in Central America, Belize has a rich culture the resembles her sister Caribbean islands like Jamaica, Barbados, Cuba, Haiti, and St.Vincent. Even though English is the official language, Kriol(broken English), Spanish, Garifuna, Mopan, and Ketchi. As a member of the CARICOM and Mesoamerica community, Belize deserves to be explored.
